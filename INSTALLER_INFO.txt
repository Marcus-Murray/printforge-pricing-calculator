Welcome to PrintForge Pricing Calculator Setup
===============================================

This wizard will install PrintForge Pricing Calculator on your computer.

PrintForge is a professional pricing calculator for 3D printing businesses,
featuring:

  - Comprehensive cost calculations
  - Excel export for professional quotes
  - Client management system
  - Quote templates and material presets
  - Automatic backups
  - Print profiles and batch quoting

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
-------------------
  - Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
  - 200 MB free disk space
  - 4 GB RAM recommended

WHAT WILL BE INSTALLED
----------------------
  - PrintForge application
  - Desktop shortcut (optional)
  - Start menu entry

YOUR DATA
---------
All your data (clients, quotes, settings) will be stored in:
  C:\Users\<YourName>\AppData\Roaming\PrintForge

This data is preserved when you uninstall or update the application.

Click Next to continue, or Cancel to exit Setup.
